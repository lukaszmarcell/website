About me

I accidentally got to know music-making programs when I was 12 years old. I was the kid how downloaded everything from the internet without caring about viruses. If I remember correctly, I downloaded a game but in its folder, there was a browser and another program that I had no idea what it was. I opened it and I saw that it had tracks and little icons resembling musical instruments. I looked it up on the internet and learned how to use it.

I have always tried my hand at songwriting but my first songs weren't really the best ones. I had nothing to write lyrics about. Then in the summer of 2021 my heart got broken by a girl I was fully in love with. I really couldn't process what happend and then I started writing songs. And when I showed my first songs to my friends and they liked it, it made me feel cheerful and alive again.
